The Co-Operative Ghost Hunting VR Game!

Phasmophobia is an online 4 player co-op horror with VR support. Paranormal activity is on the rise and it is up to your team to investigate and end the nightmare.

Intense 4 Player Co-op

Whether using VR or just a PC, form a team of up to 4 with your friends or other Ghost Hunters in online multiplayer with full dedicated servers.

Immersive Gameplay

Immerse yourself in a realistic experience that will get your heart pumping. Using fully interactive ghost hunting equipment your team will work together to investigate several hauntings that will change each time you play.

Haunting Locations

The game will offer several locations during early access in which you can expect to hunt ghosts through several houses, a prison and an asylum with more to come.

Procedural Ghosts

The game offers fully procedural ghosts meaning each game will be different. This will require you and your team to do some detective work to find out what the ghost is and how to deal with it.
